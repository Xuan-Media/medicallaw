About the Conference

INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON MERGERS AND ACQUISITIONS (M&A) IN THE HEALTHCARE SECTOR 2025 - HIMA 2025 (Held for the first time) organized by Medicallaw, will bring together more than 200 leaders from ministries, healthcare corporations, investment funds, biomedical technology and genetic testing, as well as enterprises in cosmetics, nutrition, dairy, and international experts.
The event aims to promote legal transparency and standardize M&A processes in the healthcare sector, with a vision toward IPO readiness and sustainable growth.
