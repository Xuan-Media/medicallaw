About The Conference

The 1st Vietnam Healthcare M&A Conference 2025, hosted by Medicallaw, brings together 200+ leaders from ministries, healthcare corporations, investment funds, and international experts. The event aims to create transparency and standardize the M&A process in the healthcare sector, paving the way for IPOs and sustainable development.
