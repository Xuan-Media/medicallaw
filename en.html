About the Conference

The INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON M&A IN THE HEALTHCARE SECTOR 2025, organized by Medicallaw, brings together over 200 leaders from government ministries, healthcare corporations, investment funds, biomedical and gene-testing technology firms, as well as companies in cosmetics, nutrition, dairy, and more, alongside international experts.
The event aims to enhance legal transparency, standardize M&A processes in the healthcare sector, and pave the way for IPOs and sustainable development.

Some typical projects and hospitals that Medicallaw has advised, accompanied, and provided legal support for in the healthcare sector
